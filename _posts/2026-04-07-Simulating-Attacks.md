Through my research of what employers and hiring managers are looking for in a cybersecurity candidate is actual experience with the tools and situations of the trade. So, to further my experience and improve my skills in the art of incident response and penetration testing I took it upon myself to create a HackTheBox (HTB) account and have started to go through the machines they have. Through this journey I will be creating chapters in this project detailing where I am at on my HTB journey and any cool or interesting information or experiences I encounter along the way.
<!--more-->

## Starting Point

As the title of this section says this is the starting point for all new accounts on HTB. This guides you through basic and fundamental concepts of cybersecurity. Which includes sub-sections Foundations, Fundamental Exploitation, and Multi-Step Attacks and Privilage Escalation. In each of the sub-sections there are free and 'premium' machines that you can exploit. Due to me not wanting to pay for the premium account status I will be limited to only the free machines for now.
